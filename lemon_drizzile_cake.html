Lemon Drizzle Cake

About the Recipe:

Preparation Time :15 Mins

Cooking Time :45 Mins

Level of Difficulty:Easy

Number of Servings:6

Ingredients:

  1. 225g unsalted butter
  2. 180 gms caster sugar
  3. 4 eggs
  4. 1 lemon,zested
  5. 2 lemons,juiced
  6. 65 gms caster sugar

Recipe for the cake:

Pre-heat the oven to 180 deg cel. Beat together the butter and caster sugar until creamy,then add the eggs,one at a time,slowly mixing through.

Shift in the self-raising flour,then add the lemon zest and mix until well combined.

Line a loaf tin(8 X 21 cm) with greasesproof paper,then pour in the mixture and level the top with a spoon.

Bake for 45 to 50 mins.
